Here are some instructions on how to use it to its best potential.


What is Daialog?

Daialog is a teacher helper agent that will helps students to learn languages without the necessity of a teacher. You are able to ask the agent any type of question regarding language learning. For this prototype, you will only be able to talk to it in English. The idea is making it scalable to any language.


Some commands:

(Voice commands are allowed only on google chrome)

Daialog is a conversational AI, therefore it will try it's best to understand what you say/type. You can keep it as natural as you want and he will answer you his best, if you keep on hitting a wall, and he doesn't quite understand what you are trying to say, turn to this cheat sheet that indicates what phrases he is guaranteed to answer.


Ps: Be creative, you can change words to your liking.

Meaning of word request:

    What does lamp mean?
    Bottle defenition.

Usage of word request:

    How to use regardless?
    How would you use the word therefore?

Why use a word request:

    When to use whom?
    How to correctly use garbologist in a sentence?

    How to pronounce stew?
